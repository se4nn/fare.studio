Tembo

October 2020

When Tembo were starting out they approached us to help to define their product, strategy and brand.

Tembo's goal is to bring better access to financial knowledge across generations of family members. They are building products that allow families to work together, as a unit to get the best rates on financing. To build the trusted platform for family lending.

We worked with the founding team at Tembo to define their MVP. Building on work already done by the team, we defined the best route to market and designed the journey for their first customers to flow through.

Launching in October 2020 Tembo are building the platform for family lending, allowing access to cheaper consumer borrowing rates.
